## Universal Issue Themes

- **Cross-package contract drift**: producer-side schema or payload changes landed before the matching consumer/runtime layer was ready. Examples: `fluentform/fluentform#916`, `WPManageNinja/fluent-conversational-js#40`, `fluentform/fluentformpro#170`.
- **Output sanitization and escaping**: translated/rendered output paths introduced unsafe HTML injection risks in preview, email, or shortcode rendering. Examples: `fluentform/fluentform#914`, `fluentform/multilingual-forms-fluent-forms-wpml#6`, `fluentform/multilingual-forms-fluent-forms-wpml#7`.
- **Accessibility and keyboard handling**: focus traps, mouse-only controls, unlabeled actions, and keyboard-inaccessible editing surfaced repeatedly in admin and runtime UIs. Examples: `fluentform/fluentform#904`, `fluentform/fluentform#912`, `fluentform/fluentform#914`.
- **Failure-state and loading-state dead ends**: admin interfaces often handled happy paths only, leaving retries, disabled controls, or stale status views on failure. Examples: `WPManageNinja/fluentforms-pdf#12`, `WPManageNinja/fluentforms-pdf#13`, `WPManageNinja/fluent-player-dev#319`.
- **Authorization and scope enforcement**: several reviews pushed on route/body target mismatches, write-path validation gaps, and permission checks that were weaker than the read-path constraints. Examples: `fluentform/fluentform#821`, `fluentform/fluentform#869`, `WPManageNinja/fluentforms-pdf#18`, `fluentform/fluentformpro#147`.
- **Background job correctness and idempotency**: migration, import, subtitle, and async media flows raised concerns around locks, replay safety, indexing, and forward progress after partial failure. Examples: `fluentform/fluentform#821`, `WPManageNinja/fluent-player-dev#310`, `WPManageNinja/fluent-player-pro#76`.
- **Hot-path performance and cache reuse**: repeated form lookups, N+1 queries, and repeated global string registration were flagged when added inside frequently executed render or submission flows. Examples: `fluentform/multilingual-forms-fluent-forms-wpml#2`, `fluentform/multilingual-forms-fluent-forms-wpml#6`, `fluentform/fluentformpro#141`.
- **Data integrity and normalization**: grouped options, translated labels, ranking values, and crop/upload flows repeatedly needed stricter normalization between edit-time and submit-time data shapes. Examples: `fluentform/fluentform#912`, `fluentform/fluentformpro#168`, `fluentform/multilingual-forms-fluent-forms-wpml#7`.

## Universal Tracker Fields

- `Category`: contract-drift, output-sanitization, accessibility, state-handling, authorization, async-correctness, performance, or data-integrity
- `Evidence`: PR link plus `autharif` review submission/comment timestamps
- `Status`: blocked, commented, approved-after-fix, or approved-no-blocker
- `Concern`: a repo-agnostic summary of the problem, not a plugin-specific implementation note
